Nova Lox Bagel

Description

A lox bagel is an open-faced sandwich made up of cream cheese and a fillet of brined salmon called lox topped with veggies, capers, and fresh dill.

Ingredients

*Completely Customizable
  • lox or smoked salmon
  • Everything Bagel or other
  • cream cheese
  • cucumber
  • red onion
  • capers
  • salt and pepper
  • lemon juice and fresh dill
    •

    Steps

  • Toast bagel, spread each half with cream cheese
  • Top the bagel with lox/salmon, cucumber, red onion, capers, salt, pepper, lemon juice, and fresh dill
